Cognitive Surplus is an eclectic podcast featuring real conversations with writers, thinkers, and producers striving to go places yet unexplored.

Daniel Spitz is an interactive fiction author. His first game, Niney, won an Audience Choice ribbon at the 2017 Spring Thing Festival. His current project is Venience World, a text-based game with a new kind of parser interface. He is interested in finding new ways to convey inner experience. He lives in Somerville, MA.
