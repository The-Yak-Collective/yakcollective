An essay series aiming to explore the future (or lack thereof) of the nation state. In this series we will examine the legacy of the Westphalian nation-state, its relationship to other early-modern polities from other parts of the world, post-modern forces acting on it today, and thelongue durée futures that might emerge past the events I'm writing about in the companion Great Weirding series, which focuses on contemporary events.
