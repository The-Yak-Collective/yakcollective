# Regular Expressions

## Metacharacters and Repetitions

`\d` - `[0-9]``\D` - `[^0-9]``\w` - `[0-9a-zA-Z_]``\W` - `[^0-9a-zA-Z_]``\s` - Whitespace characters (including line breaks)`\S` - Non-whitespace characters

NOTE: Be aware that `\w` _includes_ `_` (but not `-`)!
