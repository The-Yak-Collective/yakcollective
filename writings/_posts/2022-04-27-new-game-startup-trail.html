We just launched Startup Trail a new browser-based game where you explore policy issues facing tech startups. Found your company and then navigate a series of thorny dilemmas as you grow.

This game was developed as a collaboration between Leveraged Play, Copia Institute, and Engine, as a playful way to explore the issues in Engine’s report Startup Agenda.

We had a blast making this game and we hope you enjoy playing it (and learning something, too).

If you’re interested in commissioning a similar-style game, definitely reach out: randy@leveragedplay.com
